About

Baishakhi Ganguly is an expert Odissi dancer.She started her dancing career at the age of 18 under expert guidance of guru Dona Ganguly. (Guru Dona Ganguly is an accomplished odissi dancer and a student of Guru Kelucharan Mahapatra,an icon in the field of Indian contemporary dance).Currently she is attached to Techno India School as a dance teacher.She is also founder of Rhythm dance academy school where she mentors young talents of India.

Baisakhi has performed in many prestigious programs in India and abroad and has won many accolades. Below are the list of programs where she has been noted for her excellent performance.

  • Uday Shankar festival (India)
  • Doordarshan Kala Kendra (India)
  • Eastern Zonal Cultural center (India)
  • Maihar festival (India)
  • Sitapur Mahotsav in Lucknow (India)
  • Sankat mochan and Kanha utsav in Varanasi (India)
  • 8 Sanjukta Panigrahi Award Ceremony and Mukteshwar festival (India)
  • Konark festival (India)
  • International dance festival in Bhubneshwar (India)
  • Triveni Mahotsav in Allahabad (India)
  • Haldi Utsav (India)
  • Banga Sammelan in North American Bengali Conference (Detroit and San Francisco)
  • Anando Utsav (London)
  • World Expo in Shanghai (China)

She has won many prestigious awards for her perfomance. Below is the list of awards bestowed to her for her exemplary performance.

  • Winner of Doverlane Music and Dance conference award in 2008(She has been chosen topper in all categories and best qulaifying dancer)
  • Winner of Sanjukta Panigrahi memorial dance competition held in Aurobindo ashram
